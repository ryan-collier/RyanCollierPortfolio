P.01

NFL Temporal Language Shift Analysis

Analysed 5 decades of NFL commentary to test whether vocabulary drift from the 1970s to the 2010s hurts a classifier's ability to predict player position across eras. Bootstrap log-ratio shift analysis and rolling TF-IDF lexical divergence quantified the drift; within-decade vs. cross-decade classification (Naive Bayes, Logistic Regression, Linear SVM) showed macro-F1 dropping from ~0.80 to ~0.30 across decades, confirmed significant by a McNemar test.
